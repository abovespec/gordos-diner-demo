Our Story

A Vaughan Institution

Originally launched in 2007 as Wimpy's Diner, Gordo's is a staple in the Vaughan community known for original specials, fantastic service, and legendary soups. Our 1950s-60s themed dining room — complete with checkered floors, red booths, neon lighting, and jukeboxes — brings a warm, nostalgic atmosphere to every meal.

Owned by James Seol and Min Joo, Gordo's is their fourth restaurant venture, built on a simple belief: "Our staff makes Gordo's Gordo's."
